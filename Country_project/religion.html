The Importance of Islam in Saudi Arabia

Islam is the foundation of life in Saudi Arabia. It influences the country's culture, laws, values, and daily routines. Saudi Arabia is home to the two holiest cities in Islam: Mecca and Medina, which attract millions of Muslims every year for pilgrimage.

Mecca – The Holiest City

Mecca is the birthplace of the Prophet Muhammad and the location of the Kaaba, the most sacred site in Islam. Every Muslim around the world faces the Kaaba when they pray.

Pilgrimage (Hajj and Umrah)

Each year, millions of Muslims travel to Mecca to perform Hajj, which is required once in a lifetime for able Muslims. Umrah can be performed at any time of the year.

Medina – The City of the Prophet

Medina is where the Prophet Muhammad established the first Muslim community and where he is buried today. The Prophet’s Mosque is one of the largest and most beautiful mosques in the world.
